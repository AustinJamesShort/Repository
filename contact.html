About Me

Hello, I am Austin James Short. I am a driver, thrill-seeker, explorer, tinkerer, web designer and developer, editor, writer, and straight-shooter. I love everything that has an engine, including my 1994 Mazda Miata 5-speed. I live to venture to new places, especially in the mountains of California. I like making one-on-one connections with interesting people, listening to their stories and learning what makes them unique.

I enjoy creating professional, simple and highly-functional websites, for business or pleasure. I enjoy the high-risk/high-reward nature of web development, where a single mistake can cause a catastrophic failure. I thrive in a fast-paced environment, and I do my best work when time is short. I appreciate the simpler things in life, which is reflected in my minimalistic designs. I believe the simpler a design is, the more genuine and honest it will appear.
